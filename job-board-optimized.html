About Hired Teens

A job board built for the first rung of the ladder.

Hired Teens connects high school students with local businesses, nonprofits, and community organizations offering part-time, after-school, and summer roles. Finding a first job can be confusing, and a thin resume shouldn't stand between a student and a good opportunity — so we built a board where students can search, and employers who want to hire students can post directly to them.

We keep listings simple and honest: real hours, real pay, and a clear description of what the job actually involves, so students know what they're applying for before they apply.

For students

Search and filter roles by type and location, see the details up front, and apply right from the listing — no account required.

For employers

Post a role in a couple of minutes and reach students actively looking for their first job, part-time work, or a summer gig.
