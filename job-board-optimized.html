About Hired Teens

Lucas NickelsburgFounder

Hired Teens was created by New York City high school student Lucas Nickelsburg after seeing how hard it was to find a summer job in New York City. Online job boards like Indeed or ZipRecruiter don't cater to teenagers, and they don't usually have information about internships. NYC's government-run Summer Youth Employment Program helps connect students with some employers (you can apply for it here), but it only has enough slots for 20% of NYC teenagers and mainly operates only during the summer. Hired Teens aims to serve the other 80% by connecting teenagers directly with job and internship opportunities in the summer and in the school year.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Hired Teens ("we," "us," or "our") collects through this job board, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using this site, you agree to the practices described here.

Who this applies to

Hired Teens connects students with businesses and organizations offering part-time, after-school, and summer opportunities. This policy covers both employers who post listings and students who browse them.

Information we collect

From employers posting a listing: business or organization name, contact email, an optional application link, job details (title, location, pay, description), and payment information processed securely by our payment provider, Stripe — we never see or store full card numbers ourselves.

From students browsing listings: we do not require an account to search or view job postings. If you choose to apply to a listing, you'll contact the employer directly by email or through their own application link — Hired Teens does not collect or store your application materials.

How we use information

  • To publish and display job listings on the board.
  • To process payment for job postings via Stripe.
  • To respond to questions sent through our Contact page.
  • To maintain and improve the site, and to detect or prevent misuse.

Payment processing

All payments for job postings are handled directly by Stripe, a third-party payment processor. Stripe's own privacy policy governs how they handle payment details. We only receive confirmation that a payment succeeded, not your full card information.

Cookies and tracking

Hired Teens does not currently use cookies, advertising trackers, or third-party analytics.

Children's privacy

Hired Teens is intended for use by students who are at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Job listings themselves are posted by employers, and applying to a listing happens directly between the student and the employer, outside of Hired Teens.

Data retention and deletion

Job listing information is retained for as long as the listing is active, and may be kept afterward for record-keeping. Employers or students with questions about specific data, or requests to have information removed, can reach out via our Contact page.

Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. Job listing details (business name, description, contact method) are, by design, publicly visible to anyone browsing the board. We share payment details only with Stripe, solely to process transactions.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
